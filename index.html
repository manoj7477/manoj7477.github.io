About Me

As a driven individual from rural Bengal, Manoj Kumar Jana, junior researcher at AIIMS-Delhi, India. Jana transformed from a learner to a leader by overcoming linguistic barriers to excel in biotechnology. Manoj Kumar Jana is the President and Founder Of JUST ONE STEPFOUNDATION also Founder and CEO at BioFolk-AI and the visionary behind JUST ONE STEP FOUNDATION (NGO). With a strong background in immunology, virology, molecular biology, and data analysis, Jana is dedicated to bridging the gap between fundamental research and real-world applications. As a Fellow of the American Society of Microbiology, Jana actively contributes to advancing scientific knowledge and fostering global research collaborations. Through BioFolk, he is committed to empowering researchers with cutting-edge analytical tools, world-class training programs, and comprehensive guidance for academic and professional growth.
